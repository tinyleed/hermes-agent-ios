# Code of Conduct

Hermes Agent iOS follows a simple rule: be direct, respectful, and useful.

## Expected behavior

- Be constructive in issues, reviews, and discussions.
- Assume good intent, but be precise about risks and tradeoffs.
- Keep security reports private until resolved.
- Avoid harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may moderate issues, pull requests, and discussions to keep the project focused and safe.
